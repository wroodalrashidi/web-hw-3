Top Desserts

Zenzai

Japanese zenzai is an unusual dessert which combines thick red bean soup with mochi, the famous sticky rice cake.

Chiacchiere

These Italian carnival pastry fritters are made from dough that is cut into thin, rectangular sheets, which are then twisted into ribbons.

Lokma

This internationally known, decadent, and sugar-packed dessert is usually made with a mixture of flour, sugar, yeast, and salt, which is deep-fried.

Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is a decadent Brazilian dessert made by heating three key ingredients together - unsalted butter, cocoa powder, and condensed milk.
